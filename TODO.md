### Long-Haired Breeds
- Persian – flat face, silky coat, calm and gentle
- Maine Coon – large, tufted ears, dog-like personality
- Ragdoll – goes limp when held, very docile and blue-eyed
- Norwegian Forest Cat – thick double coat, built for cold climates
- Siberian – muscular, semi-hypoallergenic, affectionate
- Birman – silky coat, white "gloves" on paws, social
- Turkish Angora – elegant, often white, highly intelligent
- Balinese – long-haired Siamese, vocal and playful
- Somali – long-haired Abyssinian, fox-like appearance

### Short-Haired Breeds
- Siamese – vocal, sleek, pointed coloring, very social
- Bengal – spotted/marbled wild look, energetic, loves water
- Abyssinian – ticked coat, athletic, curious
- British Shorthair – round face, plush coat, easygoing
- American Shorthair – classic tabby look, adaptable
- Russian Blue – green eyes, grey-blue coat, shy but loyal
- Burmese – silky, people-oriented, playful
- Tonkinese – Siamese × Burmese cross, social and chatty
- Egyptian Mau – naturally spotted, fastest domestic cat
- Ocicat – wild-looking spots, fully domestic temperament
- Bombay – all black, like a miniature panther
- Havana Brown – chocolate-brown coat, rare
- Chartreux – French breed, blue-grey, quiet and gentle

### Unique / Specialty Breeds
- Scottish Fold – folded ears, round face, calm
- Munchkin – very short legs, otherwise normal cat behavior
- Sphynx – hairless, wrinkled, extremely warm and affectionate
- Devon Rex – curly coat, large ears, mischievous
- Cornish Rex – wavy coat, slender, very active
- LaPerm – curly/wavy coat, gentle and affectionate
- Selkirk Rex – curly plush coat, laid-back
- American Curl – ears curl backward, playful into adulthood
- Turkish Van – loves water, bold color patches on head and tail
- Manx – naturally tailless or short-tailed, robust
- Japanese Bobtail – short pom-pom tail, lucky symbol in Japan
- Pixiebob – wild bobcat look, loyal like a dog
- Savannah – serval hybrid, very tall and active, needs space
- Chausie – jungle cat hybrid, athletic, rare

### Colorpoint Breeds (Siamese-derived)
- Himalayan – Persian body + Siamese coloring
- Colorpoint Shorthair – Siamese with non-traditional point colors
- Snowshoe – white paws + Siamese points
